Content

Home page/ MENU

The home page of my bakery website would be visually appealing and informative, providing visitors with a quick overview of my business and what I offer. I will use high-quality photos of baked goods to showcase the products and attract new customers. A welcome message for my customers to read and to know what my website is about will be a great and warm start to making purchases. The welcome message will be:

“Welcome to Crumbs & Co., where the aroma of freshly baked goodness fills the air and every bite is a delightful symphony of flavors. Our passion for baking is evident in every creation, from our classic breads and pastries to our innovative treats that tantalize the taste buds. Step into our warm and inviting space, where the art of baking is celebrated and the sweet aroma of happiness lingers. Here at Crumbs & Co., we pride ourselves on using only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, to craft our delectable treats. Our commitment to quality is unwavering, ensuring that every bite is a burst of pure satisfaction. We believe that baking is an art form, and our skilled artisans meticulously handcraft each creation with passion and precision.”

In addition to my bakery description and welcoming message, the home page will also include a prominent menu of what we sell, including descriptions, photos, and prices. This will allow visitors to easily browse my selection of baked goods and start building their cart.

Shopping cart

In this section, the customer will see a list of what they have added to their cart, which is where they can review their selections and finalize their order. It will include a cart summary, providing a clear and concise breakdown of the items in the cart, including the quantity, price, and any applicable taxes. We will offer our customers a variety of payment options, such as credit card, debit card, and PayPal. And after that, when they have completed their purchase, a message will appear telling them the date and time they can go pick up their purchase at the counter.
